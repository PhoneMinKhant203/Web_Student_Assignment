Our Mission

The Dhamma Digital Library was founded with the goal of making Buddhist wisdom accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free. We believe that spiritual knowledge should not be hidden behind paywalls.

What We Offer

  • Digitized Pali Canon (Tipitaka)
  • Modern commentaries by respected teachers
  • Guided meditation audio guides
  • Multilingual translations

Our Values

Generosity (Dana)

All resources are 100% free of charge.

Wisdom (Panna)

We provide authentic and verified texts.

Peace (Santi)

Our platform is designed for quiet study.
