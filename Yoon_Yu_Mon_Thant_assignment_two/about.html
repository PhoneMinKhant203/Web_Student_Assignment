Reaching the Unreachable

Myanmar's mountain regions are beautiful, but their isolation makes healthcare a challenge. RuralCare is a network of volunteer doctors and local leaders working to bridge that gap.

Our goal is to ensure that even the smallest village in Chin or Shan state has access to life-saving information and basic medical supplies.

What We Do

  • Mobile medical clinics in remote villages.
  • Health education workshops for local elders.
  • Distribution of first-aid kits and clean water tabs.
